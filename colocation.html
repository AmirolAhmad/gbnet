COMPLETE HVAC FACILITIES FOR YOUR SERVER

Heating, Ventilating, and Air Conditioning (HVAC) technology of automotive environmental comfort is applied throughout the design and development of the Data Centers. You can be assured that your Servers are well kept in the most conducive environment for maximum effeciency and life-span.

Flexible Storage Space

A standard 1U vertical usable space, measured at 1.75 inches height, 19 inches width, and 36.5 inches depth will be at your deployment. Typically, you can install one piece of rack-mount equipment in this allocated 1U rack space. The sliding rails can lock in various extended positions to prevent the equipment from moving if extended out from the rack for service. This rack design permits easy server installation and removal. A KVM switch is used to share a single keyboard/video/mouse set amongst many different servers.

Dedicated Bandwidth

A dedicated 10Mbit/s Internet Connection, with 30% bandwidth allocated for International access and 70% bandwidth allocated for Local access, is immediately ready for you to do great things on the Internet once your Server is plugged-in. Network monitoring tool is at your perusal to help you determine the ideal settings for your website traffic, with the flexibility to upgrade or downgrade the Internet bandwidth if necessary.

Convenient Locations

Our Data Centers are strategically located at the central of Kuala Lumpur district, and it is also available at the hub of Information and Telecommunications at the Cyberjaya district. Coupled with our easy to use online scheduling system, you will find that a visit to the Data Centers to upkeep your Server is a pleasurable event. Furthermore, all the chosen Data Centers are known for their state-of-the-art facilities. You will be impressed with what's offered.

Best Fit For Your Server

You bring in your favourite rack-mount Server, regardless of brands, we will provide you the top class facility for it to shine. Although bearing different names, all Servers in the market are manufactured with a standard list of requirements and measurements, and we are the fore-front runner in preparing our rack enclosure with strict adherence to all industry standards.
Thus, your server will fit perfectly to the designated rack like it is a destiny

Always on Power Allocation

Your Server at the designated rack space will be equipped with an always-on power allocation. It is our observation that a typical Server normally consumes 80 to 90 watts per hour for continuous operations. Even though the Server goes through many cycles of power usage, peak vs. off-peak periods, throughout the day, you will be amazed that our facility
is optimized to accommodate your power requirements. You Server is always on, period.

1st Level Support

The 1st level support will be included in the Co-Location package as we perform basic server monitoring and handling such as checking the output from the server screen, pressing on/off switch to reset the Server, and swapping a faulty cable. Optional Server Management service is available to provide you with a comprehensive list of service options. Our support phone is always guarded by a professional who is dedicated and experienced. In addition, the email ticketing system and online knowledge portal are always available if you have any questions.

Fixed IP Address

You will be given a fixed IPv4 address for your Server. With a dedicated and static IP address, you do not have to worry about any downtime every time the IP address refreshes. If there is a need for eCommerce, you can easily setup your own Private SSL Certificate. This static IP address also provides the flexibility for you to remote access the Server and troubleshoots issues. Moreover, you can set it up as an FTP server for file transfer or direct access the Server through fixed IP address.

Well Ventilated

On top of the regulated temperature of 20°C (±2°C) and humidity of 50%rH (± 5%rH), we have arranged the Server rack into rows of hot and cold aisles, so that the mixing of air in the data center is minimized. Racks are also populated with the heaviest and most power-dense equipment at the bottom to give that equipment the best access to the coldest air. We setup containment to provide complete segregation of the hot and cold air streams, which has the benefit of reducing energy use in the HVAC system.

All-Around Protections

All our Data Centers are in compliance with Global Carrier standards. The facilities are under 24x7 Customer care, enhanced facility security and safety with centralized monitoring system, powered by dual power distribution system backed by UPS and generators in N+1 configuration. It is also setup with early fire detection and FM200 suppression system, plus it is a guarded building with secured door access and CCTV surveillance system.
